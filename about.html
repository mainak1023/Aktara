About Aktara

Celebrating India's rich textile heritage through premium handwoven sarees crafted with love and precision since 1985.

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Aktara began as a small family business with a passion for preserving India's traditional textile craftsmanship. What started as a humble venture has grown into a trusted name in premium handwoven sarees.

Our journey is rooted in the belief that every saree tells a story - of the artisan who weaves it, the tradition it represents, and the woman who wears it. We work directly with skilled weavers across India to bring you authentic, high-quality sarees that celebrate our rich cultural heritage.
